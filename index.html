    ABOUT IQUEST

    The club envisions a society with better knowledge, awareness, and access to technologies around the globe. We believe in achieving solutions of real world problems through using technologies which are either developing, new or old in an innovative way. The key idea is to provide an environment where people can interact, present their ideas, discuss it and modify it for application in our day to day life.
    And, it's not always research because we do conduct many National Level Workshops and fun events like Minute-2-Win-it. Our membership drive is free and accessible to journals, internships and many more enthusiastic events.

    ABOUT VIT

    Vellore Institute of Technology(VIT) , has been consistently ranked in the list of “Top 15 Engineering Universities” by the India Today Magazine. It is one of the premier institutions in the country. VIT University accommodates over 20,000 students spread over 20 undergraduates, 34 postgraduates, 4 integrated and 4 research programmes. There are total 2 fests held in VIT in a year which are – ‘Gravitas’ which is a technically oriented festival, while ‘Riviera’ is a cultural fest, ranked as one of the finest Indian college fest. VIT University’s main campus is in Vellore, which is spread over 250 acres of land and its other campuses are in Chennai, Amaravati & Bhopal.

    WORKSHOP

    Competitive Coding Workshop is being organized by Innovators Quest and Coding Blocks.

    Competitive Coding is a programming competition that involves the host presenting a set of logical or mathematical problems to the contestants (who can vary in number from tens to several thousand), and contestants are required to write computer programs capable of solving each problem. Judging is based mostly upon a number of problems solved and time spent on writing successful solutions, but may also include other factors (quality of output produced, execution time, program size, etc.)

    It is recognized and supported by several multinational software and Internet companies, such as Google and Facebook.
